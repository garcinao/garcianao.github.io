Vodka:

Rooftop Lemonade

Ingredients

  • 2 oz. vodka, preferably Belvedere
  • 3 oz. fresh squeezed lemonade
  • cucumber slices
  • lemon wedges for garnish

Preperation

Shake vodka and lemonade. Toss with cucumber slices. Pour into a Collins glass and garnish with lemon wedges. Vodka:

Vodka Gimlet

Ingredients

  • 2 oz. vodka
  • 1 oz. fresh lime juice
  • 3/4 oz simple syrup
  • lime wedge for serving

Preperation

Shake vodka, lime juice, and simple syrup. Pour over a coupe and garnish with lime wedge.

Ginger Basil Grapefruit Spritzer

Ingredients

  • 1 grapefruit: plus additional grapefruit for garnish
  • 2 cups of ice
  • 3 x 12oz cans of ginger soda
  • Handful of fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup basil simlpe syrup

Preperation

Juice one large grapefruit and segment additional grapefruit for garnish if preferred. Fill a pitcher halfway with ice. Pour soda fresh grapefruit juice and simple syrup. Mix well. Garnish with grapefruit slices and fresh basil.

Basil Simple Syrup

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup loosley packed fresh basil

Preperation

Place sugar and water in a small pan over medium heat until sugar is fully dissolved. about 2 minutes. Add the basil. Allow to steep for 15-20 minutes. and then strain and discard the basil. Place syrup in a glass tupperware container.Cover and chill for a couple of hours.

Virgin Mojito

Ingredients

  • 1 sprig fresh mint leaves
  • 1 tablespoon simple syrup (equal parts boiling water and sugar. Cool before using)
  • 1/2 sliced lime
  • Sparkling water
  • Mint leaf for garnish

Preperation

Muddle the lime and mint leaves (if you prefer to, remove the lime skin first) Combine the muddled mixture with simple syrup Place ice cubes in a ball glass, add mixture Top with sparkling water and stir Garnish with a mint leaf and serve
